How much does house cleaning cost in Carlsbad, CA?
House cleaning in Carlsbad, CA starts at $169 per visit for weekly recurring service, $189 per visit for biweekly, and $218 per visit for monthly. One-time deep cleans and move-out cleans are priced based on home size. Call (619) 374-6213 for a custom quote in minutes.
What is the difference between a standard clean and a deep clean?
A standard clean covers routine maintenance — vacuuming, mopping, wiping surfaces, cleaning bathrooms, and tidying kitchens. A deep clean goes further: baseboards, ceiling fans, grout lines, inside appliances, window sills, and hard-to-reach areas. Most new clients start with a deep clean, then switch to recurring standard cleans.
What does a Carlsbad deep clean include?
Our signature Home Reset deep clean includes detailing and hand-wiping baseboards, door frames, and trim; deep-cleaning kitchen window tracks and sill areas; scrubbing and degreasing exterior and interior major appliances; sanitizing bathroom tile grout, shower surrounds, and fixtures; wall-to-wall dusting including ceiling fans and light fixtures; and vacuuming, edging, and deep mopping hard flooring surfaces.
How do I book a house cleaning in Carlsbad, CA?
Fill out the online quote form or call (619) 374-6213. We respond fast, confirm your price, and schedule your clean — no lengthy phone calls required. Most clients get a price and book in under 5 minutes.
Are your cleaners insured and background checked?
Yes. Every provider in the Maid Nice San Diego network is fully insured and passes a comprehensive criminal background check before entering any home. Providers are also regularly performance-vetted, and we are Fully Insured & Vetted.
How does your referral agency model work?
We vet the top independent cleaning professionals across San Diego County and match them to your job — handling the logistics, quality guarantees, and customer support so you get a trusted local provider without the hassle of finding one yourself.
What areas near Carlsbad, CA do you serve?
Maid Nice San Diego serves Carlsbad, Encinitas, Oceanside, Vista, San Marcos, and Escondido, California. If you are outside these areas, call us and we will confirm availability.
Will I get the same provider every visit?
Yes. Weekly and biweekly recurring clients are matched with the same provider every visit. This ensures consistent quality and lets your provider learn your home's specific preferences over time.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
No. Most clients provide a spare key, garage code, or door code. We clean while you are at work or running errands and send before-and-after photos when we are done.
What is included in a standard house cleaning?
A standard clean includes: dusting all surfaces, wiping countertops, cleaning bathroom fixtures (toilet, shower, sink, mirrors), vacuuming all floors, mopping hard surfaces, and cleaning exterior appliance surfaces. All cleaning supplies are included. We do not clean inside ovens, fridges, or cabinets in a standard clean — those are available as add-ons.
Do you bring your own cleaning supplies and equipment?
Yes. We bring all supplies and equipment to every job. You do not need to provide anything. If you have a preferred product or a sensitivity to certain chemicals, let us know when booking.
Do you offer move-out cleaning in Carlsbad, CA?
Yes. Our move-in/move-out cleaning service is designed to get your home deposit-back ready. It includes deep cleaning inside cabinets, drawers, appliances, and every corner. Flat-rate pricing is available — call for a quote.
Do I need to sign a long-term contract?
No. We offer flexible one-time deep cleans, move-out options, and recurring services with absolutely no contracts required. Cancel, pause, or reschedule at any time with no penalty.
What if I am not satisfied with my clean?
We offer a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. Contact us within 24 hours of your clean and we will return the next business day to re-clean any missed areas at no charge. No hassle, no excuses.
How do I know if I need a deep clean or a recurring clean?
If your home has not been professionally cleaned in the last 4–6 weeks, or if it is a first-time clean, we recommend starting with a deep clean. After that, a recurring standard clean every 1–2 weeks keeps it maintained. We can advise you on the right plan when you call.
How long does a house cleaning take?
A standard clean for a 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom home typically takes 2–3 hours. A deep clean of the same home takes 4–5 hours. Timing varies based on home size, condition, and the specific services requested.
